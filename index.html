Someone New, Lew

You already know the usual names. My goal is not to be the third version of the same thing—it’s to bring a different perspective. I’m running because I believe ordinary Nevadans should have at least one option on the ballot who is not owned by the usual machine.

Concerns

Cost of living, housing, schools, mental health, seniors on fixed incomes, waste and confusion in state agencies—these are not talking points to me. Over the coming months, this section will grow with specific concerns I’m hearing from Nevadans and what I think we can do about them.

Failures

Both parties have had their turn at the wheel. Both have things to answer for. This campaign is willing to say plainly where our government is failing and who is responsible—without pretending that one party has all the answers.
